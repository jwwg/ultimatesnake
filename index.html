How to Play

Controls:

  • Use arrow keys to control the snake
  • Press Space to start the game
  • Press P to pause

Game Rules:

  • The game is over after 5 hands, or if you hit yourself
  • Collecting cards will grow the snake
  • Avoid hitting yourself. The snake can wrap around the edges of the screen.
  • When a hand is completed, a bird will fly across the screen. Catch it to increase your multiplier!
  • Complete poker hands (current hand at the bottom of the screen) to score
